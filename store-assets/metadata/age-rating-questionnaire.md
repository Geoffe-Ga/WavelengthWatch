# Age Rating Questionnaire — WavelengthWatch

Answers to the App Store Connect Age Rating questionnaire. Enter these values
exactly when configuring the app in App Store Connect.

**Target rating**: **4+**

## Additional Disclosures

- **Made for Kids?** No.
- **Kids category?** No.
- **Intended for children under 13?** No — but content is appropriate for 4+.

## Notes for Reviewer

If Apple flags "Medical/Treatment Information" as elevated, respond with:

> WavelengthWatch does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical
> condition. The self-care strategies are general wellness suggestions drawn
> from widely available contemplative and somatic practices. The app includes
> no HealthKit integration and no clinical claims.
